## What is PSI?

PSI is a web-based engineering platform for **pressure vessel support design and analysis**. It guides mechanical and structural engineers through the full lifecycle of a support calculation — from geometry input to printable reports — all within a modern browser dashboard.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account and log in">
    Register at `/registro` and sign in to access the full platform dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter vessel geometry">
    Navigate to **Support Design** and fill in outer diameter, length/height, wall thickness, insulation, fluid level, and specific gravity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure loads">
    Set wind zone, exposure category, and seismic parameters (site class, Ss, S1, or COVENIN city/state/soil type).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and configure a support type">
    Choose from saddle, skirt, legs, lug, or ring support — each with its own geometry panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review analysis and export">
    Check the analysis dashboard for metric results and charts, then print the design summary report.
  </Step>
</Steps>
